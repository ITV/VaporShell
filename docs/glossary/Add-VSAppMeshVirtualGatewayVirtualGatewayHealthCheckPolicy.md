Add-VSAppMeshVirtualGatewayVirtualGatewayHealthCheckPolicy [[-Path] <Object>] [-UnhealthyThreshold] <Object>
 [[-Port] <Object>] [-HealthyThreshold] <Object> [-TimeoutMillis] <Object> [-Protocol] <Object>
